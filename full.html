TXT record

TXT is a DNS record that provides text information to sources outside your domain. With Google Cloud services, TXT records are used to verify domain ownership and to implement email security measures such as SPF, DKIM, and DMARC.

CNAME record

CNAME is a DNS record that links an alias name to another true or canonical domain name. For instance, www.example.com might link to example.com. Here the CNAME record is used to verify domain ownership.
